Technology
for Learning

Performance Learners do not have to wait for scheduled ‘computer time’ on shared devices. Instead, with a personal device, students have just-in-time access to a powerful tool that allows them to drive and document their learning in a way they never could before.

Beyond
the Classroom

By virtue of being out of the school setting, students on overnight field trips learn to be ambassadors for their school, their community and even their country. They learn the realities of travel, the communication skills and leadership skills necessary for teamwork.
